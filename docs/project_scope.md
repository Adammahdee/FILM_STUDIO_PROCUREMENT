# 2. PROJECT BACKGROUND

Film production organizations regularly purchase equipment, props, costumes, lighting equipment, audio equipment, production materials, transportation services, and post-production resources.

Many studios still rely on spreadsheets, emails, paper-based approvals, and disconnected inventory records.

These approaches often result in:

* Slow approval processes
* Procurement delays
* Inventory inaccuracies
* Poor visibility of stock levels
* Lack of accountability
* Missing audit trails
* Duplicate purchases
* Difficulty generating reports

The Film Studio Procurement Approval and Inventory Management System is designed to centralize procurement activities, automate approval workflows, manage inventory, and provide management reporting within a single platform.

---

# 3. PROJECT GOAL

To develop a centralized web-based system that manages procurement requests, approval workflows, inventory assets, suppliers, purchase orders, notifications, and reporting for film studio operations.

# 4. PROJECT OBJECTIVES

2. Automate approval workflows.

4. Maintain accurate inventory records.

5. Manage supplier information.

6. Generate purchase orders.

7. Record inventory transactions.

8. Maintain activity logs.

9. Provide management reports.

10. Improve operational efficiency.
